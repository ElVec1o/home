Content Types

Writeup: Full break analysis with TL;DR, prompts, model response, and considerations. Use for detailed documentation.

Post: Short notes, quick observations, links. Less formal than writeups.

X Post: Embed your tweets. Just paste the URL.

Workflow

1. Open admin.html locally in browser
2. Create content (Writeup/Post/X Post tabs)
3. Go to Export tab
4. Click "Download JSON"
5. Replace src/data/breaks.json
6. Git commit & push
7. Site rebuilds automatically
